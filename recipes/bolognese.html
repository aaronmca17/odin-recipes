Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

  • Ground Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Wine

How to make:

  1. Sauté aromatics: In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté finely chopped onions and minced garlic until softened
  2. Cook the meat: Add ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork) to the pan. Cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks
  3. Add wine: Pour in a splash of red wine and let it cook off, scraping up any bits from the pan
  4. Simmer the sauce: Stir in crushed tomatoes, a pinch of salt, pepper, and any desired herbs (like basil or oregano). Let the sauce simmer for 30–45 minutes to thicken and develop flavors
  5. Serve: Cook the spaghetti according to package directions. Top with the Bolognese sauce and enjoy with a sprinkle of Parmesan
