Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast or Thighs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic

How to make:

  1. Sauté aromatics: In a large pan or wok, heat oil and sauté chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant and softened
  2. Cook the chicken: Add sliced chicken breast (or thighs) to the pan. Cook until browned and cooked through, stirring occasionally
  3. Add sauce: Pour in a mix of soy sauce, oyster sauce, and a splash of rice vinegar. Stir well to coat the chicken
  4. Add vegetables: Toss in your choice of vegetables (like bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, or snap peas). Cook until tender-crisp, stirring frequently
  5. Serve: Serve the stir-fry over steamed rice or noodles for a quick, flavorful meal
