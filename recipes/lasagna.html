Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps on how to make:

  1. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water according to the package instructions, usually around 8-10 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce: In a large skillet, cook ground meat (beef, pork, or a combination) over medium heat until browned. Add marinara or tomato sauce and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, seasoning with salt, pepper, and herbs like oregano or basil.
  3. Mix the ricotta filling: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with a beaten egg, salt, pepper, and any desired herbs (such as parsley or basil). This will be your cheesy layer.
  4. Assemble the lasagne: In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom. Then, layer the cooked noodles on top, followed by more sauce, a layer of ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers, finishing with a top layer of noodles, sauce, and mozzarella.
  5. Bake: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake the lasagna for about 25-30 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
